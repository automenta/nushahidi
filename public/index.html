Welcome to NostrMapper!

NostrMapper lets you create and view geo-tagged reports on the decentralized Nostr protocol.

Key Concepts:

  • Identity: Use a NIP-07 browser extension (like Alby) or manage keys locally (backup your private key!).
  • Relays: Data is published to and read from Nostr relays. Configure your preferred relays in Settings.
  • Focus Tags: Reports are grouped by a primary "#focus_tag" (e.g., #MyEvent). Set this to scope your view and new reports.
  • Public Data: Everything you publish to Nostr is public. Be mindful of what you share.

Explore, report, and discover!
